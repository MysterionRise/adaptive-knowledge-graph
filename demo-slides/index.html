KG-Aware RAG: How It Works

Regular RAG

Question→Semantic Search→5 Chunks→LLM→Answer

KG-Aware RAG (Ours)

Question→Concept Extraction→Graph Traversal→Expanded Query
→Hybrid Search (20)→Cross-Encoder Rerank→Top 5→LLM→Answer
KG adds concept extraction + graph traversal before retrieval. Cross-encoder reranker filters 20 → 5 chunks by semantic relevance to the query.
